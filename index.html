BIM Department Manager
Abdallah Zakaria
Head of BIM for Mimar Group, overseeing Mimar Architecture & Engineering and Mimar Plus.
  • Set BIM strategy and standards for both companies, aligned with business goals and client requirements.
  • Oversee BIM Managers, BIM Leads, Senior BIM Coordinators, BIM Specialists, BIM Coordinators and the BIM Software Developer.
  • Approve and maintain organizational BIM standards, templates, BEP/MIDP/TIDP frameworks and quality procedures.
  • Ensure alignment with ISO 19650 principles for information management and use of the Common Data Environment (CDE).
  • Govern CDE usage, information workflows, and digital collaboration platforms across all projects.
  • Manage departmental KPIs, performance reviews, training programs and capability development for the BIM team.
  • Drive technology adoption and innovation, including automation, scripts, and integration with other digital tools.
  • Coordinate BIM practices across Mimar Architecture & Engineering and Mimar Plus to maintain consistency and quality.
  • Report BIM performance, risks and strategic opportunities to executive leadership.
